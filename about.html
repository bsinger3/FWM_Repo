About Friends With Measurements

← Back to search

Friends With Measurements helps you find clothing that actually fits by showing you real product photos from people with body measurements similar to yours. Instead of guessing whether a dress or pair of jeans will look good on you, you can see how it looks on someone with your exact height, weight, bust, and hip measurements.

How it works

We collect product photos and sizing information from public retailer websites where shoppers share their measurements alongside photos of themselves wearing the clothing. You can search by entering your height (in feet and inches), weight (in pounds), bust size (in inches), and hip size (in inches). Our system then finds photos from people with similar measurements to yours.

What "similar measurements" means

When you search, we match your measurements against our database to find the closest matches. We prioritize finding people with similar overall proportions rather than requiring exact matches on every dimension. This means you'll see results from people who are built like you, even if one or two measurements differ slightly.

Who this is for

Friends With Measurements is designed for anyone who struggles with online shopping and wants to see how clothing looks on real bodies before buying. Whether you're petite, plus-size, tall, or have proportions that don't match standard sizing charts, our tool helps you find visual references from people with your body type.

Data sources

All images and measurements come from publicly available product review pages on major retailer websites. We aggregate this information to make it searchable in one place. When you click through to a product, you'll be directed to the original retailer page where you can purchase the item.

Privacy and accuracy

We only display information that shoppers have already chosen to share publicly on retailer websites. We do not collect or store any personal information from our users. Measurements are self-reported by the original reviewers, so accuracy may vary.
