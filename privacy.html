Privacy Policy

FriendsWithMeasurements shows product photos and sizing details collected from public retailer pages. We do not store user account data or payment information.

What we collect

When you use the site we may collect basic analytics such as page views, referrers, and browser information via Cloudflare Web Analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising.

Images and links

Images are loaded from retailer sites through our image proxy for speed and reliability. Clicking a product link may use an affiliate redirect to help support hosting costs.

Your choices

If a retailer or rights holder wants a specific image or link removed, email support@friendswithmeasurements.com and include the exact URL.
