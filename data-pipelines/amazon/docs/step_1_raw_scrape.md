# Step 1 Raw Scrape

Step 1 stores the raw Amazon scrape inputs.

What belongs here:

- untouched scrape CSVs
- raw upstream files that have not yet been standardized
- raw files that still reflect source-specific layouts

What does not belong here:

- normalized outputs
- machine-annotated image review files
- human-reviewed approval sheets

Current examples include the raw scrape CSVs moved from the legacy raw scrape
area and the Snowflake-style raw review scrape.
